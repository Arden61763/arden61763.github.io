Charity Colouring Competition

Posted on 26th May 2019

We're glad to announce that on 8th June 2019 we will be starting a colouring competition to aid Children in Need! All pupils will be provided with a colouring sheet with a character of their choice. Crayons will be provided but if you have other materials you'd like to use, such as paint or glitter, these are also allowed. We look forward to seeing what masterpieces are created by our pupils!
These sheets must be returned by 15th June 2019 and winners will be announced in assembly on 16th June 2019, the day after.

Summer Break

Posted on 22nd July 2018

We've now broken up for the summer holidays! We hope that you all have a pleasant summer and that you make the most of the time off school.
Most importantly, we hope you stay safe during the break. If you're going out in the sun, make sure you're covered to protect yourself from sunburn. Be careful if you're swimming and always make sure your parents know where you are!
School will start again on 3rd September 2018, but for now - have fun!

Term Dates 2019/2020

Posted on 2nd July 2018

We have received the new term dates for 2019/2020 and have got these up on the website for all to see! They are available on our Term Dates page at https://arden61763.github.io/termdates.html.
If you have any concerns or issues with these term dates, please don't hesitate to contact us at attendance@ape.sch.uk, or phone us on 0121 111 111. Please remember that your child's attendance is crucial to their education and as such, all absences must be approved ahead of time where possible.
