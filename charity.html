Charity 2019

We're glad to say that so far, the progress on our charity drive for the year has raised over £850 towards our goal of £1,000! With our Charity Colouring Competition coming later this month, we are sure to beat our overall target easily! You can read more about the upcoming Colouring Competition here.
At the end of the year, a vote will be held among the students about what cause to donate the proceeds to, with the chosen charity receiving all money raised for the year!
